CERTIFICATE OF INSURANCE

Certificates of Insurance (COI) are available upon request to qualified customers under NDA.
COIs will name the requesting customer as an Additional Insured for general liability claims.

To request a COI, contact our legal team at legal@acmesaas.com with:
- Company name and contact information
- Purpose of COI request
- Any specific requirements (e.g., waiver of subrogation)

COIs are typically issued within 5 business days of request.

POLICY UPDATES AND RENEWALS

- Errors & Omissions: $5M minimum

Customers will be notified if coverage amounts change or policies are non-renewed.
